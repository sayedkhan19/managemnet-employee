Management Employee
This project is designed to efficiently manage employees, their work, and payments within an organization. It supports three distinct user roles: Admin, HR, and Employee — each with specific permissions and responsibilities.

Admin: Has full control over the system. The admin can perform all actions, including hiring and firing employees, managing HR and employee accounts, and overseeing all operations.

HR: Can view detailed employee information, validate employee records, and manage monthly salary payments for employees. HR ensures that employee data is accurate and payments are processed on time. 

Employee: Can view their own work details and payment information.
